Beaver Hacks

  • Registration

    Page where participants go to register for the hackathon or update their information. List of participants is displayed and can be searched and sorted, and participants can be deleted.

  • Teams

    Page where participants or administrators add, update, and delete teams. List of teams is displayed and can be searched and sorted. Participants can added and removed from teams.

  • Projects

    Page where participants or administrators add, update, and delete projects. List of projects is displayed and can be searched and sorted.

  • Admin

    Page for administrators to add, update, and delete hackathons. List of hackathons is displayed and can be searched and sorted. Many-to-many relationships may also be added and deleted from this page.
