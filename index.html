Beaverhacks

  • Participant Registration

    Page where individuals go to register for the hackathon or to update their contact information. Participants can also be deleted from this page.

  • Teams and Projects

    Page where participants create teams or add themselves to existing teams. The page is also where projects are added, and links to project's github, devpost, and video are added after project is complete. Teams and projects can also be deleted from this page.

  • Admin

    Page for administrators to add, edit, delete hackathons. The page is also for admins to add, edit, delete judges.
