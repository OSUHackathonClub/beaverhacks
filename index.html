Beaver Hacks

  • Registration

    Page where participants go to register for hackathon or update their information. List of participants for current hackathon is displayed and can be filtered to see participants for other hackathons. Participants can also be deleted from database from this page.

  • Teams

    Page where participants and administrators can add and delete teams. List of teams for all hackathons is displayed. Participants may be added and removed from teams.

  • Projects

    Page where participants or administrators add and delete projects. List of projects for all hackathons is displayed.

  • Admin

    Page for administrators to add or delete a hackathon. Also where current hackathon is set. List of all hackathons is displayed.
