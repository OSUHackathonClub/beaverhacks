Beaverhacks

  • Participant Registration

    Page where participants go to register for the hackathon or update their contact information. List of participants is displayed and can be searched and sorted.

  • Teams and Projects

    Page where participants or administrators add, update, and delete teams and projects. Lists of teams and projects are displayed and can be searched and sorted.

  • Admin

    Page for administrators to add, edit, and delete hackathons and judges. Lists of hackathons and judges are displayed and can be searched and sorted.
